Comedy Prague was formed in April 2013 to provide regular English comedy in Prague. From only producing occasional shows, Comedy Prague now runs regular monthly shows in different venues around Prague.

In 2016, Comedy Prague wants to continue developing by producing new shows in different parts of Prague, the Czech Republic and internationally. Comedy Prague is made up of experienced and dedicated performers from all over the world.

Comedy Prague also runs workshops to train new comedians and has trained over 15 comedians in 3 years. Comedy Prague also runs workshops to train comedians in improv comedy and other aspects of comedy performance.
