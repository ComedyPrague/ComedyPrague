Comedy Prague was formed in April 2013 to provide regular English comedy in Prague. From producing only occasional shows, Comedy Prague now runs several monthly shows in different venues around Prague.

Comedy Prague is made up of experienced and dedicated performers from all over the world.

Comedy Prague also runs workshops to train new comedians in improv comedy and other aspects of comedy performance.
