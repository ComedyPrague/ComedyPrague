Phil Micallef is an English person stuck in a weird limbo state between music and comedy. He thinks music is a joke, but takes comedy very seriously, and so he has decided to find a neat compromise between the two. The result is a gag-punctuated gallivant of songs about various relatable hard-hitting issues, such as Czech politics, low-cost airlines and loving a kebab a little bit too much.
