Originating from the UK, stand-up comedian and MC Tori Burton has found herself finally making some sense of the hot mess that is her life in Prague. From immigration to lap dance, paralysis to the paranormal, problem solving to pooping, Tori speaks on her experiences with conviction and honesty.
