Lukáš comes from Brno, which is to Prague what Thebes were to Athens. He likes to tell jokes that nobody gets, not even himself. Thebes, is that a brand of margarine?

He's tall, but likes shorts. His favorite comedians are George Carlin, Steve Martin and Miloš Zeman.
