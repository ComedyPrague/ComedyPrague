Born and raised in deepest darkest East London, Russell Adam Webb is a blogger, comedian, nerd, tea expert and all around geezer. Developing in comedy and trying new stuff is what he's all about.

Influenced by George Carlin and Louis CK, Russell likes to look at the ridiculousness of life, love politics and especially religion.
