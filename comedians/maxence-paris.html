Maxence Paris is a comedian from France. He used to work as an assistant director on feature films in France and Belgium. In October 2015, he moved to Prague with his wife and was subsequently promoted as Head of Cleaning and Cooking Duties at home. He performs stand-up with Comedy Prague to release the stress involved with his new position and to hear the voices of other human beings.
