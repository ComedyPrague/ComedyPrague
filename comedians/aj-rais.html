AJ Rais was born between the mountains and the deserts of Afghanistan in the years of the Soviet occupation. He speaks English, Persian, Arabic and Camel. He managed to escape a Taliban execution, because they caught him rend-handed with Czech beer, which he smuggled into Afghanistan.
