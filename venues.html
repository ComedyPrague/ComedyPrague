Venues

Where can you find us?
Every 1st Friday of the month in The ACT Prague
Every 3rd Wednesday of the month in A Maze in Tchaiovna
Every other Thursday in Redroom Music Bar

The ACT Prague

Na Královce 7, Prague 10
Every 1st Friday of the month we run comedy showcase with a special host as the headliner in this cool and cozy theatre in Vršovice.

A Maze in Tchaiovna

Muchova 4, Prague 6
Every 3rd Wednesday of the month we run an open mic A Maze in Tchaiovna - a combination teahouse/theatre/gallery/venue & bar and a true community center.

Redroom Music Bar

Myslíkova 28, Prague 1
Every other Thursday of the month we run showcase in Redroom, a vibrant pub in the very center of Prague.
